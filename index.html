I work at the intersection of structured matrices (HSS/HBS, low‑rank formats) and optimization for neural networks (mass matrices, Gauss–Newton/Hessian structure), with an emphasis on turning theoretical structure into fast, reliable solvers.

I am on the postdoc job market this year. My interests span numerical linear algebra, scientific machine learning, and fast direct/iterative methods.

Research

My research focuses on hierarchical low‑rank representations and their role in accelerating training for two‑layer ReLU networks. I study the structure of mass and Gauss–Newton matrices and develop linear‑time factorization/solve routines under realistic accuracy targets.
